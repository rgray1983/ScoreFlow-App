# ScoreFlow Future Ideas

This file captures approved future directions that are not yet assigned to an implementation PR.

## Scheduling and operations

- Team calendar and agenda views
- Recurring practices
- Multi-day tournaments
- Pool-play and bracket matches
- Player availability
- Practice and match attendance
- Arrival times and travel notes
- Transportation coordination
- Court assignments
- Cancellations and rescheduling
- Calendar sync with Google, Apple, and Outlook
- CSV and common schedule imports

## Team communication

- Coach announcements
- Parent and player notifications
- Event reminders
- Read receipts for important team updates
- Coach-controlled sharing and privacy

## Player development

- ESPN-style player cards
- Achievements and milestones
- Development goals
- Coach-reviewed progress summaries
- Recruiting profiles and shareable graphics

## Match and analytics

- Video synchronization
- Heat maps and shot charts
- Rotation success analysis
- Opponent scouting
- Momentum and serving-run insights
- AI-assisted post-match summaries and practice suggestions

## Platform growth

- Multi-team club dashboards
- Referee or table-crew mode
- Sponsor graphics
- Branded public team pages
- Advanced exports and integrations

Ideas become implementation work only after they receive a focused PR scope and their effect on existing systems is documented.
