# Rotations

## Purpose

Create a visual volleyball teaching and match-preparation tool that coaches can save, animate, analyze, and share.

## Planned capabilities

- Half-court or extended-court board
- Drag players into zones
- Save named rotations and lineups
- Add movement arrows and annotations
- Save multiple steps
- Animate steps like a coaching whiteboard
- Connect rotations to teams, seasons, and roster memberships
- Share as an image, PDF, or portal item

## Future analytics

- Side-out percentage by rotation
- Points won and lost
- Serve-receive rating
- Weak rotation alerts
- Rotation-specific notes and opponent scouting

The creator must be touch-first on iPad and remain usable on a phone without requiring precise mouse-style control.
