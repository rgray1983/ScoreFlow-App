# Players and Rosters

## Player profiles

Player profiles are reusable across seasons and teams where permissions allow. Planned fields include name, photo, contact details, dominant hand, graduation year, height, reach, vertical, notes, and optional recruiting information.

## Roster memberships

A roster membership connects a player to a team and season and stores season-specific information:

- Jersey number
- Position
- Active, injured, inactive, graduated, or guest status
- Captain or leadership designation
- Season-specific notes
- Portal visibility

## Player cards

Premium player cards may include headshot, team branding, jersey number, position, season stats, recent performance, achievements, and shareable social graphics.

## Design rules

- Do not duplicate the full player profile for each season.
- Historical roster records remain intact after jersey numbers or positions change.
- Private contact and coach notes are never exposed in public or parent/player views without explicit permission.
