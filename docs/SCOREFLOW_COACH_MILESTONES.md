SCOREFLOW Volleyball Coach Platform Master Vision + Feature Plan Built
around the existing ScoreFlow scoreboard style, live-match actions, and
mobile-first experience.

Core direction: Volleyball only first. Coach-focused. Mobile and iPad.
Offline-first. Integrated with the ScoreFlow live scoreboard. 1. Product
Vision ScoreFlow Volleyball Coach expands the current ScoreFlow
Scoreboard into a complete volleyball coaching platform. The standalone
scoreboard remains the live scoring and broadcast engine, while
ScoreFlow Coach becomes the daily workspace for coaches, assistants,
players, and parents. ScoreFlow Scoreboard - live scoring, viewer links,
fan zone, broadcast ticker, match results, and shareable graphics.
ScoreFlow Coach - rosters, teams, clubs, rotations, live stat tracking,
player development, practice planning, reports, and match analysis.
ScoreFlow Team Portal - player and parent access to team stats, player
stats, match history, results, and selected coach-shared content. The
app should feel like ScoreFlow: clean cards, bold sports UI, red and
gold action hierarchy, thumb-friendly mobile controls, and clear
coach-first flows. It should be more powerful than a simple scoreboard,
but easier and less intimidating than traditional volleyball stat
software. 2. Competitive Inspiration: iStatVball 3 iStatVball 3 is a
strong reference point for the level of volleyball depth that serious
coaches expect. Its public feature set includes RallyFlow touch
recording, shot charts, heat maps, play-by-play, real-time stats,
momentum charts, video sync, cloud backup, MaxPreps/Bound integration,
custom reports, PDF/Excel exports, rosters, lineup building, and
organization by school/club, age group, and season. Source:
https://istatvball.com/ The opportunity for ScoreFlow is not to copy
that product feature-for-feature. The opportunity is to combine
volleyball depth with the ScoreFlow experience: faster entry, better
visual design, a cleaner live broadcast layer, parent/player access, and
a daily coach workspace that feels simple from the first tap. iStatVball
is primarily a statistics app. ScoreFlow should become a volleyball
platform. Depth matters. Coaches need real stats, not a toy version.
Simplicity matters more. In a match, the app cannot slow the coach down.
Experience is the differentiator. ScoreFlow already has live viewing,
reactions, ticker graphics, and shareable results that make people want
to watch and share. 3. Core Product Principles 4. Main App Modules Coach
Hub Home base for today: upcoming match, live match, roster, rotations,
practice plan, recent results, and stat shortcuts. Teams & Clubs Create
clubs with multiple teams and seasons. Support school programs, club
programs, JV/Varsity, and age groups. Rosters Player name, number,
position, height, handedness, grad year, contact fields, status, notes,
and photo. ESPN-style Player Cards Headshot with background removal,
player number, position, team color, season stats, recent match stats,
and shareable profile graphics. Live Match Hub Scoreboard controls, stat
entry, current rotation, timeline, substitutions, timeouts, and match
status in one place. Two-Touch Stat Tracking Tap player card, then tap
stat. Add undo, quick correction, and recent event timeline. Rotation
Creator Half-court visual board where players can be dragged into place,
arrows can show movement, steps can be saved, animated, and shared.
Practice Builder Drag drills into a practice timeline, save plans, and
connect plans to stats-driven areas of improvement. Reports & Analysis
Match reports, season stats, trends, rotation success, side-out
percentage, serving runs, and player development charts. Parent / Player
Portal Read-only access to team schedule, match results, player stats,
player cards, team stats, and shared rotation/practice content. Offline
Sync Engine Local-first event storage with background sync when
connection returns. Coach Timeline A full event story of the match:
points, kills, aces, errors, substitutions, timeouts, notes, and set
winners. 5. Team / Club System The structure should support a single
coach with one team, but also scale up to an entire club or school
program. Organization: school, club, or independent program. Teams:
Varsity, JV, 14U, 15U, 16U, etc. Seasons: 2026 school season, 2027 club
season, summer league, tournaments. Staff roles: owner, head coach,
assistant coach, statistician, manager, parent viewer, player viewer.
Roster management: active, injured, inactive, graduated, guest player.
Branding: team logo, team colors, home court, default scoreboard
settings. 6. Player Profiles and Player Cards Player profiles should
feel premium, visual, and shareable. This is one of the areas where
ScoreFlow can become something parents and players love, not just
something coaches use. Player photo/headshot upload. Automatic
background removal for ESPN-style player cards. Jersey number, position,
height, reach, vertical, dominant hand, grad year, and notes. Season
stats, career stats, recent match stats, awards, achievements, and
milestones. Shareable player graphics for social media. Parent/player
view with optional coach-controlled visibility. 7. Live Match Hub The
Live Match Hub is the heart of ScoreFlow Coach. On iPad, it can show
scoring, stat entry, rotation, and timeline at once. On phone, it should
use fast tabs or swipeable panels while keeping the top scoreboard
control visible. iPad Concept Top bar: compact live scoreboard controls
connected to the ScoreFlow viewer scoreboard. Left panel: active
roster/player cards. Center panel: large stat buttons and current event
workflow. Right panel: match timeline, undo, substitutions, timeouts,
and current rotation. Bottom: quick tabs for Stats, Rotation, Timeline,
Notes, and Share. Phone Concept Top: compact scoreboard strip with set,
score, and live status. Main: player grid or stat buttons depending on
current tap step. Bottom nav: Roster, Stats, Rotation, Timeline, Share.
Undo button always easy to reach. 8. Two-Touch Stat Collection The core
live stat interaction should be simple enough to use while watching the
match, not staring at the device. Tap the player card. Tap the
stat/result button. The event records instantly, updates totals, updates
timeline, and saves locally. Example stat buttons: Kill, Attack Error,
Ace, Serve In, Serve Error, Block, Dig, Assist, Pass 3, Pass 2, Pass 1,
Free Ball, Sub, Timeout, Note. The stat system should support simple
mode first, with advanced detail added later. The app should not force
every coach to track every contact if they only want key stats. 9.
Rotation Creator The rotation creator can become one of the signature
ScoreFlow features because it connects coaching, teaching, and sharing.
Half-court or slightly extended court view. Drag and drop players onto
court zones. Draw movement arrows for serve receive, defensive movement,
transition, or base defense. Save rotation steps as Step 1, Step 2, Step
3, etc. Animate the steps like a coach whiteboard. Share with players or
assistants as an image, PDF, or team portal item. Connect rotations to
stats later: side-out %, serve receive rating, points won/lost per
rotation. 10. Coach Timeline Instead of only storing stat totals,
ScoreFlow should store the story of the match. Every key event becomes a
timeline entry. Ace by #12 Ava. Kill by #7 Maddie. Attack error by
opponent. Timeout - Blazers. Substitution. Set 1 final - Blazers 25:18.
Coach note: switched to 5-1 offense. After the match, the timeline
becomes the source for reports, corrections, highlight review, future
video sync, and AI analysis. 11. Reports, Stats, and Analysis ScoreFlow
should start with useful coach stats and then grow into deeper analysis
over time. 12. Parent and Player Portal This is a major growth
opportunity. Coaches pay for the tools, but players and parents spread
the product when they can view and share meaningful content.
Player-specific stats and progress. Team match history and upcoming
schedule. Results graphics and player cards. Coach-approved rotation
diagrams or practice notes. Read-only permissions so parents and players
cannot alter official data. Optional privacy controls for what is
public, team-only, parent-only, or coach-only. 13. Offline-First Sync
Offline support is not optional. Volleyball gyms often have weak cell
service, and coaches cannot lose match data. All scoring and stats save
locally first. Every event gets a unique local ID and timestamp. Sync
queue retries automatically when connection returns. Cloud sync updates
team, player, parent portal, and scoreboard history. Conflict handling
favors timeline/event order and keeps a correction log. Visible sync
status: Saved on device, Syncing, Synced, Needs attention. 14. AI
Opportunities AI should not replace coaching. It should surface patterns
that are easy to miss during a live match. Post-match insights: best
serving run, weak rotation, side-out drops, momentum swings. Practice
suggestions based on recent match stats. Player development summaries
for coach review. Automatic narrative recap for parents and social
posts. Scouting notes from trends across opponents. 15. Build Phases
Phase 1 - Foundation Teams, clubs, seasons, rosters, player profiles,
basic player cards, and current ScoreFlow scoreboard integration. Phase
2 - Live Match Hub Two-touch stat tracking, compact scoreboard controls,
timeline, undo, local match event storage. Phase 3 - Offline Sync
Local-first database, sync queue, cloud update, recovery, and conflict
handling. Phase 4 - Reports Match reports, season stats, player stats,
team stats, basic charts, shareable result cards. Phase 5 - Rotation
Creator Court board, drag/drop players, arrows, saved steps,
share/export. Phase 6 - Team Portal Parent/player read-only stats, team
pages, player pages, privacy controls. Phase 7 - Advanced Analytics Heat
maps, shot charts, rotation analysis, opponent scouting, AI insights.
Phase 8 - Premium Platform Club dashboards, multi-team management,
branded pages, sponsor graphics, advanced exports, video sync
exploration. 16. Monetization Thoughts The free version should keep the
core ScoreFlow scoreboard useful and shareable. The paid coach product
should unlock the full volleyball program platform. Free Scoreboard -
live score, viewer link, basic results, basic team branding. Coach Pro -
roster, stats, match reports, player cards, rotation builder, offline
sync, team portal. Club / School Plan - multiple teams, multiple
coaches, parent/player access, branding, season history, exports.
Premium Add-ons - AI analysis, sponsor graphics, advanced reports, video
sync, recruiting/player profile packs. 17. Immediate Next Steps Create a
formal app map for ScoreFlow Coach: Home, Teams, Roster, Live Match,
Rotations, Reports, Portal, Settings. Define the first roster/player
